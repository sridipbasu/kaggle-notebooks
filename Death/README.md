# The Illusion of Progress: Why America's Falling Death Rate Doesn't Tell the Whole Story

A storytelling driven analysis of US mortality data from 1999 to 2017. While the national death rate fell steadily, not every state shared equally in that progress. This notebook follows the diverging paths of Washington D.C. and West Virginia to uncover how gains against heart disease, cancer, and stroke were offset by rising deaths from accidents, suicide, and Alzheimer's disease.
